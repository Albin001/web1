INTRODUCTION

PIC 18F4580 is an 8-bit MicroController .It is the Upgraded Version of PIC16F877A . PIc18F4580 is commonly Used MicroController
. It contain internal oscillator that is not Present in PIC16F877A.PIC18 series Available in 40 pin,44 pin and 24 Pin


Following Are the Features of PIC18F4580

  • 10 bit ADC
  • ENHANCED ADRESSABLE USART
  • ENHANCED CCP MODULE
  • Self Programming
  • 32-bit Flash Memory
  • 5 Bidirectional Port
  • 4 TimerS
  • Support CAN ,SPI AND I2C Protocols
  • Ethernett Module Is Present
